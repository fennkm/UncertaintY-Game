To play, watch the cameras and observe carefully, some objects will move to a new position while you aren't looking.

Swap cameras using [SHIFT] to get a better look at the room. You can also stop the camera by using [SPACE]. You can start it moving again by hitting [SPACE] again.

While the camera is stopped you can click on an object to fire your laser at it. Be careful to only use this on enemies, incorrect shots will drain your battery. Too many and it's game over.

You'll be shown how many enemies you've destroyed and how many there are in the room in total. Destroy them all before the timer runs out to win!
